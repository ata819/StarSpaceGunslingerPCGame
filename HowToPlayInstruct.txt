This game is a reaction/quick draw based game.The How To Play is in the options setting.

When the "FIRE" text appears, press the SPACE button!
If you press it too soon, you get penalized. 
    When penalized, your character will not draw his gun and you will be unable to fire your weapon.
    You then just accept your fate.
There are 3 Levels to pass. Pass all 3 and win the game. Die and lose the game.
Each Level gets progressively harder so make sure you sharpen you skills
